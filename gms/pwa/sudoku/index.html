How to Play

Goal

Fill every row, column, and 3x3 box with the numbers 1 through 9. Each number can only appear once in each row, column, and box.

Controls

  • Tap a cell to select it. If you recently placed a number, tapping an empty cell will auto-fill that number (if valid in the box).
  • Long press or right-click a cell to open the number picker.
  • Notes mode lets you pencil in candidate numbers at specific positions within a cell.
  • Undo reverts your last move.

Difficulty Levels

  • Basic — 56 given cells (great for beginners)
  • Simple — 51 given cells
  • Easy — 46 given cells
  • Medium — 36 given cells
  • Hard — 26 given cells
  • Crazy — 21 given cells (expert only!)

Tips

  • Look for rows, columns, or boxes that are nearly complete.
  • Use notes to track possible candidates for tricky cells.
  • Conflicts are highlighted in red to help you spot mistakes.
  • Every puzzle has exactly one valid solution.
