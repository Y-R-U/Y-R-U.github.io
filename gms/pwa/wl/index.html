How to Play

Goal: Capture all cities to become the supreme Warlord.

Select: Click an army to select it — reachable tiles are highlighted.

Move: Click a highlighted tile to move the selected army there.

Attack: Moving onto an enemy army or defended city triggers combat.

Cities: Click your own city to open the management panel. Buy heroes and train units.

Income: Each city earns gold at the start of your turn. Capitals earn the most.

Heroes: Heroes command armies, gain levels, and boost combat strength.

Ruins: Send armies to unexplored ruins — you may find treasure or powerful units.

Winning: Last faction with cities standing wins.
