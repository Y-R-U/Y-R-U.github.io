Introduction

CRPG: Lands of Ascii is a mobile-first RPG inspired by classic tile-based games. Explore three towns, battle monsters in the wild, clear dungeons, gather resources, and level up 13 skills.

This game auto-saves on every level-up. Use the Save button in the Menu tab to save manually at any time.

Controls

Movement

  • Tap / Click any walkable tile to walk there automatically.
  • Your character navigates around obstacles using pathfinding.
  • Mountain tiles (grey ^ peaks) are passable but slow you to 35% speed.

Attacking

  • Tap an enemy to target it and walk toward it ‚Äî combat starts automatically when in range.
  • Tap the same enemy again (while it is your target) to view its stats card.
  • Long-press / hold (0.6s) any enemy tile to open its stat card directly.

Gathering

  • Tap a Water tile (~ waves) adjacent to you to fish.
  • Tap a Tree tile (T) adjacent to you to chop wood.
  • Tap a Stone tile (# grey rock) adjacent to you to mine ore.
  • Tap a Building or Door (= or +) adjacent to you in a town to cook food in your backpack.

Inventory

  • Open the INV tab to see equipped gear and your 28-slot backpack.
  • Tap any item to open its popup: Equip / Use / Sell / Drop.
  • Stacked consumables (food, potions) stay open after each use so you can keep eating.

Defence XP ‚Äî Three Ways

  • Slow +1 XP every time you land a hit on any enemy.
  • Fast +5 XP when you successfully block with a shield equipped.
  • Fastest XP proportional to damage taken when an enemy hits you.
Equip a shield (offhand slot) to unlock blocking and speed up Defence training significantly.

Gathering Skills Overview

All gathering skills work the same way: stand adjacent to the resource tile and tap it. The game automatically picks the best resource tier for your current level.

Your character stays in place when gathering ‚Äî you do NOT need to path to the tile first.

‚öîÔ∏è Dungeons

Dungeons are marked with a > tile on the world map. Walk on one to get the entry prompt.

How Dungeons Work

  1. Kill all regular enemies to spawn the boss (gold star ‚òÖ).
  2. Kill the boss to unlock the exit (< tile in top-centre).
  3. Walk onto the exit tile to leave with your loot.

Flee

Tap FLEE in the entry prompt to leave safely (3-second cooldown before the prompt can re-appear).

üí° Tips & Tricks

  • HP regenerates automatically ‚Äî 1 HP every 5 seconds (faster at high Hitpoints levels).
  • Equip a shield in the offhand slot to unlock blocking and speed up Defence training.
  • Monster loot scales with enemy strength ‚Äî harder enemies drop better tier items.
  • Bosses always drop a Rare or Legendary item.
  • Use the üè† Teleport Home button in the Menu tab to return to Ashvale from anywhere.
  • Long-press any enemy to see its ATK, DEF, XP, and gold drop before committing to a fight.
  • Mountain tiles slow you ‚Äî stay on roads when possible for faster travel.
  • Fishing, Woodcutting, and Mining all auto-select the best tier for your current level.
